Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

SplitThis is a fully offline app. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

Data Storage

All data you enter — sessions, participants, bills, and split results — is stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Financial or payment information

Third-Party Services

SplitThis does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Camera Permission

The app may request camera access solely to scan QR codes for importing shared sessions. Camera data is processed on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

SplitThis does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app does not require an account or any personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the app's store listing contact info.
